About Cartoonie Craft

Cartoonie Craft is a 128x128 HD Cartoon themed Texture Pack. Currently it is in its alpha stage & I am completing it as I have time. Cartoonie Craft will only support versions of Minecraft that are 1.6 and newer since this pack has the
new Resource Pack Formatting.

Over time there will be new textures added and some textures edited. Please note, this is no where near final and textures may change depending on feedback from everyone. There are some minor issues wit h some of the
current textures I have made that will be fixed before Beta Release.
